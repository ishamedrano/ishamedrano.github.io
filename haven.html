stray kids

Stray Kids is an 8-member South Korean boy group under JYP Entertainment. The group was created through the survival program with the same name, Stray Kids. They debuted on March 25, 2018. Their most recent album is entitled NOEASY.
bias: hyunjin, seungmin
fave song: another day & blueprint

twice

TWICE is a 9-member girl group under JYP Entertainment. The group debuted on October 20, 2015, through the survival show Sixteen. Their newest album to be released on November 12 is called Formula of Love: O+T=<3
bias: dahyun, momo
fave song: baby blue love & love foolish

enhypen

ENHYPEN is a 7-member rookie boy group that debuted on November 30, 2020. The group was formed thru the survival show I-LAND under BE:LIFT Lab. Their newest album is called Dimension:Dilemma.
bias: jungwon, sunghoon
fave song: not for sale & go big or go home

ikon

iKON is currently a 6-member boy group under YG Entertainment. One of their previous members, B.I, left and debuted as a soloist recently. Their latest single was named Why Why Why, and right now they are focusing on solo projects. Meanwhile, B.I will be having a comeback with the album COSMOS on November 11.
bias: bobby, junhoe
fave song: love scenario & flower

kep1er

Kep1er is a 9-member K-pop project girl group formed through the MNET survival show Girls Planet 999. The final lineup was announced last October 22, 2021, during the final episode of the show. For now, they are still preparing for their debut on December while frequently giving contents to their fans.
bias: youngeun, bahiyyih
