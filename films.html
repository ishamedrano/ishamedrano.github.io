Hotel del Luna is a drama featuring a hotel in the middle of Seoul whose customers are ghosts, handled by the beautiful, yet short-tempered CEO who is cursed to manage the facility. She falls in love with their new hotel manager, until they had to bid goodbyes.

This is the first ever K-drama I watched and it got me hooked since then.

Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-joo is a coming-of-age drama about student athletes pursuing their dreams despite hardships, while experiencing love in their lives.

This is such a cute drama that it was my favorite! Both of the lead actors are very charming and talented. Fun fact is that they dated for a while; however, they are not in a relationship anymore.

Law School is about students studying law, obviously, in a prestigious school where they can conduct mock trials as well. Ambitious for their goals, the friend group in the drama fought for their rights together with their Professor.

Honestly saying, I got so attached to the characters for months! The drama helped me realize how unfair life could be because of power and money.

My Name focuses on the female lead seeking revenge for his father's death. She joins the police as a mole thru her crime boss which in reality she only got used.

It's true that we really don't know what could happen in the next few moments. I got so shocked and sad with the ending to the point that it was running on my mind for days. Always choose what is right.
